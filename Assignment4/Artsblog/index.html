What We Do

Action Painting

Action painting, sometimes called "gestural abstraction", is a style of painting in which paint is spontaneously dribbled, splashed or smeared onto the canvas, rather than being carefully applied.

SKETCHES

Sketching traditionally refers to a preliminary rough type of drawing that an artist might make in preparation for either a painting or a more formal drawing (like a study). A sketch is less detailed than a study - a study may be a highly detailed rendition of something to be used in a large composition..

DIGITAL ART

Digital art is an artistic work or practice that uses digital technology as part of the creative or presentation process. Since the 1960s, various names have been used to describe the process, including computer art and multimedia art. Digital art is itself placed under the larger umbrella term new media art.

Stencil Art

Stencilling is a simple method of transferring a design using a cut-out shape onto a surface. The word stencil means the outline of an image. Using stencils is a fast and efficient way to apply an image or pattern directly onto a canvas for a mixed media painting, or onto a page in an art journal. .
